About

Rubber Management System is designed to streamline and digitalize the process of rubber farming and production. It helps you efficiently track latex collection, acid usage, sheet production, sales, inventory, and losses — all in one place

  1. Home
  2. About

Plants Make Life Better

This Rubber Management System is designed to empower small-scale rubber producers and farm managers with easy-to-use tools to streamline their daily operations. Whether managing a single farm or a small production unit, the system provides:

  • Accurate recording and management of daily latex (rubber milk) collection
  • Detailed tracking of acid and material usage for sheet production
  • Efficient inventory management of rubber sheets produced and sold
  • Comprehensive sales recording and profit analysis
  • Monitoring of productivity to minimize wastage and losses

By centralizing these functions, the system helps you stay organized, make informed decisions, and maximize your farm’s profitability — making rubber farming simpler and more sustainable.
